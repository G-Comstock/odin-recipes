Pizza Dough

  • 2-2.5 cups high protein flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt

Add 1 cup warm water and yeast to a large bowl. Allow to sit ~ 5 min until well combined.

Spoon flour into cup measure to avoid compacting, and add 1 cup to yeast and water mixture. Stir to combine into a slurry and let sit for ~ 5 min.

Spoon scond cup of flour into measuring cup and into the slurry along with 1 tsp salt. stir to combine and switch to hand when dogh becomes too stiff. continue to knead in bowl until well combined.

Turn dough out onto counter and continue kneading and dusting with as much of remaining 1/2 cup of flour as necesary to get a shiney, non sticky dough ~ 10 min.

Return dough to lightly oiled bowl, cover with plastic wrap and allow to sit at room temperature for ~2 hours. 1 hour before baking turn out onto dusted counter and devide into two equal sized peices. To form each dough piece into a ball pinch the "corners" together and roll on the counter, cover with plastic wrap and let rest.

To form into a round; press dough ball flat with finger tips until it forms a small round while liberally dusting with flour gradually stretch the dough by lifting it from one edge and then the edge oposite going around it's circumferance until 9-10 inches in diameter.

Place dough round onto hot pizza stone, add sauce, toppings, and cheese return to hot oven and switch to high broil for ~ 5 min. Monitor the whole time as it will cook quickly.
