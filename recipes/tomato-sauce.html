Tomato Sauce

  • 6 plum tomatos
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp salt

Plunge tomatoes in boiling water for 30 seconds, remove and plunge into ice water. Cut tomatoes length wise, remove seends and dice.

finely chop 3 cloves of garlic, add oil to a hot pan over medium heat, add ~ 1 tbs oilive oil and garlic. Stir briefly and immediatly add tomatoes.

Add salt and continue to cook over medium heat, stirring occaisionally until tomatoes break down. ~ 15 min.
