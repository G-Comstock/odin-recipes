Bechemel

  • 2 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg

Put butter into a hot pan over medium high heat. As soon as butter melts, but is not yet foaming add flour.

wisk to combine butter and flour. Allow to cook for 1-2 minutes to combine, but do not allow to brown.

Add milk in ~ 1/4 cup incriments while whisking to prevent clumping. while continuing to whisk add salt and nutmeg.

Continue to heat wile whisking untill bechemel thickens to desired amount.
